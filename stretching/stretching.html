Welcome to the Stretching Main Page!

This is the main page for all stretching activities. You can find various stretching styles, videos, and resources here.

  • Dynamic Stretching

    Dynamic Stretching is a series of poses that flow smoothly into one another, synchronized with your breath.

  • Static Stretching

    It can be done actively, where you apply force to increase intensity, or passively, where an external force is applied.

  • PNF Stretching

    An additional form of stretching is PNF, which stands for proprioceptive neuromuscular facilitation.
